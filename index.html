About

I don't believe in programs.
I believe in systems.

For over fifteen years, I've worked at the intersection of technology, community development, and civic leadership. From founding tech companies to advising municipalities, my focus has always been the same: building infrastructure that creates predictable, lasting outcomes.

Detroit taught me that programs come and go with political winds, but systems endure. That's why I engineer solutions designed for longevity, not headlines.
