## ECONOMIC / COST OF LIVING

- **Inflation:** CPI rose 3.1% year-over-year (BLS, Jan 2026)
- **Grocery costs:** Food prices up 25% since 2020 (USDA ERS)
- **Real wages:** Median household income $80,610 (Census Bureau, 2024)
- **Savings rate:** Personal savings rate 3.7% — near historic lows (BEA)
- **Credit card debt:** Americans carry $1.14 trillion in credit card debt (NY Fed, Q4 2025)

## HOUSING

- **National shortage:** 3.8 million housing units short of demand (Freddie Mac)
- **Rent burden:** 50% of renters spend >30% of income on rent (Harvard JCHS)
- **Homeownership gap:** Black homeownership 44.7% vs White 74.5% (Census)
- **Detroit vacant lots:** 70,000+ vacant lots in Detroit (Detroit Land Bank)
- **Construction costs:** Up 38% since 2020 (Turner Building Cost Index)

## SMALL BUSINESS

- **Formation:** 5.5 million new business applications in 2024 (Census Bureau)
- **Failure rate:** 20% fail in year 1, 50% by year 5 (BLS)
- **Access to capital:** 43% of small businesses couldn't access needed funding (Fed Small Business Survey)
- **Black business survival:** Black-owned businesses 3x more likely to be denied credit (Fed Reserve)

## WORKFORCE

- **WIOA spending:** $3.6 billion annually on workforce development (DOL)
- **Skills mismatch:** 87% of employers report difficulty filling positions (ManpowerGroup)
- **Training ROI:** Only 37% of workforce trainees employed in trained field at 1 year (GAO)
- **Unemployment disparity:** Black unemployment 5.6% vs White 3.2% (BLS)
- **Job openings:** 8.8 million open positions nationally (JOLTS, BLS)

## AI & TECHNOLOGY

- **AI adoption:** 72% of companies have adopted AI in at least one function (McKinsey)
- **Job displacement:** 300 million jobs globally could be affected by AI (Goldman Sachs)
- **Digital divide:** 24 million Americans lack broadband access (FCC)
- **AI investment:** $200+ billion in global AI investment in 2025 (Stanford AI Index)

## CHILDCARE & FAMILY

- **Childcare cost:** Average $10,853/year per child (Child Care Aware)
- **Childcare deserts:** 51% of Americans live in childcare deserts (CAA)
- **Workforce impact:** 57% of parents have turned down work due to childcare (Rapid Survey, Census)
- **Single parent households:** 23% of U.S. children live with one parent (Census)

## YOUTH

- **Youth unemployment:** 16-24 age group: 8.3% unemployment (BLS)
- **Disconnected youth:** 4.5 million young people not in school or working (Measure of America)
- **Summer jobs:** Only 35% of teens held summer jobs in 2024, down from 52% in 2000 (BLS)
- **Youth violence:** Homicide is #1 cause of death for Black males 15-34 (CDC)

## SYSTEMS & GOVERNANCE

- **Nonprofit spending:** $2.6 trillion nonprofit sector (NCCS/Urban Institute)
- **Government trust:** 22% of Americans trust federal government (Pew Research)
- **Fragmentation:** Average city has 30+ workforce/social service providers with no coordination (Brookings)
- **Funding cycles:** 70% of grant-funded programs end when funding ends (Urban Institute)

## TRANSPORTATION

- **Commute burden:** 45% of jobs in metro areas not accessible by transit in 90 min (Brookings)
- **Car dependency:** 85% of Americans drive to work alone (Census ACS)
- **Transit investment:** U.S. spends $50B/year on highways vs $20B on transit (CBO)
- **Detroit transit:** 26% of Detroit households have no vehicle (Census)
